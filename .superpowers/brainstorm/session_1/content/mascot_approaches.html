Mascot Design & Integration Approaches

Meet Lando, your friendly 3D farmer guide! Choose how Lando will be integrated into AgriDirect.

Meet "Lando" (3D Clay Style)
1

Approach 1: Interactive Dashboard Tip Card & Guide

Lando sits in a clean, elevated card on the farmer's sales dashboard or consumer marketplace home screen. He gives dynamic, personalized tips inside a speech bubble (e.g., "Great job! You made 3 sales today!").

Pros: Non-intrusive, extremely helpful, guides users directly on their main workspace.
2

Approach 2: Milestones & Gamification Popups

Lando appears during major user actions and milestones. E.g., when posting a new product, finishing a crop harvest step, or reaching a sales milestone. He pops up in a cheerful celebrating state with confetti.

Pros: High emotional impact, rewards good user behavior, makes the app feel alive.
3

Approach 3: Floating Assistant Chat Bubble

A persistent, small floating badge of Lando in the corner of the screen. Tapping him opens a friendly helper sheet/overlay with quick actions, links to help documentation, or a mini-chat helper.

Pros: Always available, consolidates help features into one neat visual anchor.
